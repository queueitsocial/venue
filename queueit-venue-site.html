How it works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

Your staff doesn't need training. No new hardware. Just a QR code and your existing bar setup.

1
📲
A friend queues a drink
Someone picks your venue in the app, chooses a drink from your menu, and pays upfront. Their friend gets a push notification instantly — with your bar's photo and address.
2
🚶
They walk in and show the code
The recipient opens Queue It, shows the QR code to your bartender, and gets their drink. No app needed for you — just a phone camera to scan.
3
💸
You get paid, they order more
Revenue lands in your account. And nobody comes to a bar for just one drink — your average guest spends 3.2x the queued drink value before they leave.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for bar owners who don't want another complex tool to manage.

📊
Real-time dashboard
Every queued drink, who it's for, and redemption status — live. Track your top fans, revenue, and redemption rates in one place.
🔲
QR redemption — no app needed
Guests show a QR code on their phone. Your bartender scans it with any smartphone camera. No hardware, no POS integration, no new workflows.
🍾
Group queues & bottles
Friends chip in together for bigger gifts — bottles, rounds, champagne for a birthday. Higher ticket items, higher revenue per transaction.
❓
Mystery drinks
Customers queue a drink for a stranger who matches a description. Creates memorable moments and word-of-mouth buzz you can't buy.
📸
Drink posts — your bar on their feed
When guests redeem, they snap a photo and post it to their friends' feeds with your venue tagged. Every redemption is a potential social post. Free advertising, generated by the experience itself.
